A Short Biography

I am a general guy who dreams to make it big in computing world. I was born on 27th July 1998 to the most beautiful couple in the world. My name is Mandeep and I am from Nepal.

I have always been passionate about computer games and websites. I am enthusiastic to learn many programming languages. My interest is in developing games, websites, operating systems and many more. For this reason, I am in dilemma when it comes to choose a particular field in IT, however, my best like will be Game Development if I have to choose one. But I also want to take part once in every field of my interest.

Talking about my nature, I prefer low pressure mind zone to the surrounding with loads of stresses. I am a cool dude who likes to enjoy life with all the fun in every way possible.
